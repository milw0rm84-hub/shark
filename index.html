The Schedule

A Day of Wonder

07:30 AM

Private Port Transfer

Your journey begins at your doorstep. Our chauffeur service collects you for a short, scenic drive to the marina as the sun breaks over the Gulf.

08:30 AM

The Boarding Ritual

Step aboard our flagship vessel. Enjoy freshly roasted barista coffee and a briefing from our lead naturalist on the day's ocean conditions.

10:00 AM

Inner Reef Acclimatization

A gentle introduction to the water in our favorite coral gardens. Test your gear among sea turtles and tropical schools before heading to the deep blue.

11:30 AM — 02:00 PM

The Main Event

Guided by real-time aerial reconnaissance, we position the vessel for the ultimate encounter. Multiple swims with the world's largest fish in groups of no more than 10.

02:30 PM

Gourmet Deck Luncheon

Celebrate the day's encounters with a long-table lunch on the aft deck. Local king prawns, crisp salads, and chilled Western Australian wines.

04:30 PM

Homeward Cruise

Relax on the sun loungers as we make our way back to the Exmouth Marina. Your private transfer awaits for the return to your hotel.
